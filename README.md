**SafeCity AI – Smart SOS + Evidence System**

SafeCity AI is an intelligent safety platform designed to improve emergency response and public safety.
It combines instant SOS alerts, real-time location tracking, and live video evidence collection to provide faster and more effective help during emergencies.

**🚨 Problem Statement**

Women's safety and public safety remain major concerns in many cities.
Most existing SOS applications only send alerts but fail to provide real-time evidence or detailed information for authorities.

Because of this:
* Emergency response is often delayed
* Victims remain vulnerable for longer periods
* Authorities lack reliable evidence

SafeCity AI aims to bridge this critical safety gap.

**💡 Our Solution**

SafeCity AI introduces a Smart **SOS + Evidence System** that allows users to trigger an emergency alert instantly while automatically collecting and storing evidence securely in the cloud.

The system ensures that **family members and authorities receive real-time updates, location details, and video evidence** to respond quickly and effectively.

**✨ Key Features**
**🔴 Instant SOS Activation**

A single press triggers the emergency protocol and sends alerts to emergency contacts immediately.

**🎤 "HELP ME" Voice Trigger**

Hands-free SOS activation using voice recognition technology for situations where manual activation is not possible.

**📍 Precise Location Sharing**

Real-time GPS coordinates are shared with trusted contacts and authorities.

**🎥 Live Video Evidence Collection**

Automatic video recording captures evidence and uploads it directly to secure cloud storage.

**☁️ Secure Cloud Storage**

Evidence is stored using Supabase, ensuring secure and tamper-proof storage accessible to authorized personnel.

**🛣 Safe Route Navigation**

AI-powered navigation suggests safer routes using real-time safety data and community reports.

**👨‍👩‍👧 Real-Time Family & Police Tracking**

Trusted contacts and emergency responders can track the user’s live location during emergencies.
